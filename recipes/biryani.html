Biryani

Description

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Chicken or Lamb
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Yogurt
  • Spices (e.g., cumin, coriander, turmeric, garam masala)
  • Ghee or Cooking Oil
  • Water or Chicken Stock

Steps to make

  1. Wash the rice thoroughly and soak it in water for 30 minutes.
  2. Marinate the chicken or lamb with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, and spices for at least 1 hour.
  3. Heat ghee or cooking oil in a large pot and saut√© sliced onions until golden brown.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften.
  5. Add the marinated meat and cook until it's partially cooked.
  6. Layer the partially cooked meat at the bottom of a heavy-bottomed pot.
  7. Add the soaked rice over the meat in an even layer.
  8. Pour water or chicken stock over the rice until it's fully submerged.
  9. Add saffron strands for color and flavor (optional).
  10. Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid and cook on low heat until the rice is fully cooked and fluffy.
  11. Garnish with fried onions, mint leaves, cilantro, nuts, and raisins before serving.
  12. Serve hot with raita or salad on the side.
