Pizza

Description

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Pizza sauce
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Assorted toppings (e.g., pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, olives)
  • Italian seasoning
  • Garlic powder
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)

Steps to make

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C) or as directed by your pizza dough recipe.
  2. Roll out pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer dough to a pizza pan or baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  4. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  5. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  6. Add desired toppings, distributing them evenly over the cheese.
  7. Sprinkle Italian seasoning, garlic powder, and red pepper flakes (if using) over the toppings.
  8. Bake in preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
  9. Remove pizza from oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
