Lasagna

Description

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or Italian sausage
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Eggs
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper

Steps to make

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  3. In a skillet, brown ground beef or Italian sausage with onions and garlic. Drain excess fat.
  4. Add tomato sauce and Italian seasoning to the skillet, and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, eggs, Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper.
  6. Spread a layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a baking dish.
  7. Arrange a layer of cooked lasagna noodles over the sauce.
  8. Spread half of the ricotta mixture over the noodles, then sprinkle with mozzarella cheese.
  9. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, ending with a layer of sauce and mozzarella cheese on top.
  10. Cover the dish with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  11. Let lasagna cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
